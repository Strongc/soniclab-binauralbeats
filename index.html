What are binaural beats?




Binaural beats are a psycho-acoustic phenonemon.




They are produced when two audible frequencies,




very close to each other, are heard in each ear.




The difference between the two frequencies




is heard as a "beat"

What is Core Audio?




From Apple's documentation:!




"Core Audio is the digital audio infrastructure of iOS and OS X.




It includes a set of software frameworks designed to handle




the audio needs in your applications."




*




For us, we will focus on Audio Units




Audio Units let us create audio data and generate sound




More technically, we can create samples




and put them into output buffers
